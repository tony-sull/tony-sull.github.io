About

I started my career working primarily on native apps for Android, iOS, and Windows Phone but have focused on full-stack javascript for the last two years.

On the backend it's mainly been Node.js hosted on AWS for any production apps. The frontend is where it really gets fun with projects in Angular and React, and most recently Svelte and Sapper (which powers this site).

More of my energy is focused into open-source projects these days, staying close to the community on Gitter and starting a blog that will be released shortly!

I build this site heavily influenced by Dinesh Pandiyan's personal site - what better way to get a solid comparison of React vs. Svelte than by recreating a real-world project! Take a look at Dinesh's original project and the Svelte equivilent here and let me know what you think of the Svelte-ified code!
